Pasta with Chicken and Cream Recipe

Description:

Pasta is a staple in every diet, as is chicken, so combining the best of both worlds can be an excellent idea, but not before adding a bridge between the two; a good sauce.

For this recipes we will need the following ingredients:

  • 500g of screw pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of grain Sailt
  • 5 cloves
  • Oil to taste
  • 3 tablespoon butter
  • 1 chicken breast cut into squares
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 190g Philadelphia Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of consomme
  • 1/2 liter of milk
  • 1/4 of cream
  • Oregano to taste
  • 1/4 garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of Salt from Puebla
  • 1 brocoli cut in pieces

Preparation:

  1. Boil water until it boils, once done, pour the screw paste together with the cloves, a spoonful of grain salt and teaspoon of oil.
  2. In a pot, place two tablespoon of butter over low flame, once melted, fry the chicken over medium-high flame, add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Blend in a blender the Philadelphia cheese, a tablespoon of butter, the bouillon, the milk, the cream, the oregano, a little pepper and the garlic.
  4. In a separate pot, put some water, boil it at high flame until it boils, add a spoonful of Puebla salt and wait until it boils again.
  5. Empty the previously washed and disinfected broccoli. Let the water boil for 5 minutes and the turn off, wait 20 minutes and empty the water leaving only the broccoli.
  6. Once the chicken is fried, it has to be very golden, add the broccoli and stir, then pour the sauce already made and mix again, heat for 10 minutes and enjoy.
