Hamburger Recipe

Description

Hamburgers are one of the most famous foods in the world, but making a good hamburger meat is the difference in the whole dish, it's not only ground meat, its a mixture of spices and vegetables that gives it its juicy and characteristic flavor.

For this recipes we will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 6 springs of parsley
  • 3/4 ground beef neck meet
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

  1. Chop the onion and tomato into small squares, as well as the parsley
  2. Put the ground beef in a bowl
  3. Place the tomato, onion and parsley in the bowl
  4. Crack and place the egg with yolk and all in the bowl
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Mix all
  7. Make small balls of the mixture, no bigger than the palm of your hand
  8. Heat a frying pan with oil over medium heat
  9. Places the balls in the pan and flatten them a little with a spatula, so that they are flattened
  10. Allow the meet to sear on one side and then turn over
  11. After it is well cooked for at last 10 minutes, take it out and leave it on a plate to drain the oil
  12. Enjoy
